Hard Boiled Eggs

Description:

Recipe on how to make the perfect hard boiled eggs for the morning!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • A pot
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Any other seasonings!

Steps:

  1. Put about 2-3 cups of water to boil
  2. Throw in eggs for boiling, boil for about 15 minutes
  3. Let cool, then peel off shell from eggs
  4. Cut in half, then put salt, pepper, and whatever other seasonings
  5. Enjoy!
