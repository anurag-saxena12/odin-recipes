Peaunt Butter and Jelly Sandwhich Recipe

Description:

A recipe on how to make the perfect PB&J sandwhich.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Strawberry Jelly
  • Peaunt Butter

Steps:

  1. Warm bread, don't toast, in toaster
  2. Spread peanut butter on one side
  3. Spread Jelly on other side
  4. Combine sides, and now you are done!
